About Us

Who We Are

The Charity Run 2024 is an annual event dedicated to promoting health and fitness while supporting local charities in our community. Our mission is to create an engaging and fun experience for participants of all ages while raising funds to support those in need.

Our Mission

We believe in the power of community and the impact of collective efforts. Our mission is to foster a spirit of giving and support, encouraging everyone to participate in physical activities while contributing to meaningful causes.
