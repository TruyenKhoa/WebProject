General Information

How to Reach the Venue

SECC is located at 799 Nguyen Van Linh Street, District 7, Ho Chi Minh City.

Parking Information:

  • Location: Behind SECC (on Hoang Van Thai street)
  • Parking Options:
    • Motorbike
    • Car
    • Bicycle
  • Parking Fee:
    • Motorbike: 5,000 VND/hour
    • Car: 20,000 VND/hour
  • Operating Hours: 6:00 AM - 10:00 PM
  • Alternative Parking: Additional parking available at Cresent Mall (5-minute walk)

Transportation Options:

  • Public Buses: Bus No. 34 and 68 stop near SECC
  • Taxi: Available at all major locations
  • Ride-Sharing Services: Grab and Gojek operate in the area
