Participation Information

How to Participate

Select your role below to view detailed information on registration, guidelines, and fees:

Participate as a Runner

  • Register Online: Visit our registration page, choose your race category (5km, 10km, 21km), and complete the registration form.
  • Pricing:
    • 5km Fun Run: 240,000 VND
    • 10km Challenge: 480,000 VND
    • 21km Half Marathon: 720,000 VND
  • Runner's Kit: Includes a race bib, event t-shirt, and timing chip. Collect your kit 2 days before the event at SECC.
  • Guidelines: Follow all event rules, wear your race bib visibly, and arrive at least 30 minutes before the start time.

Volunteer for the Event

  • Roles Available:
    • Route Marshals: Guide and support runners along the route.
    • Hydration Station Assistants: Provide water and snacks to participants.
    • Check-In Assistants: Help with participant registration and kit distribution.
  • Volunteer Requirements: Volunteers must be 16 years or older and commit to attending a training session prior to the event.
  • Registration Process: Complete the volunteer application form online, and select your preferred role and shift.
  • Benefits: All volunteers receive a certificate, event t-shirt, and meal voucher.
